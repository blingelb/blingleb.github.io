Portfolio

Movees

The goal of this project was to take the existing design of the popular Starship delivery bots and change the mental model to create more human-to-human interaction. This project allows the user to order a Starship projector to any location on Purdue's campus and create an outdoor movie expirience. This expirience can be shared with either friends or cultivate new friendships through a shared outdoor expirience.

Value of UX Design

This exercise is intended to educate us on the values of user experience as it pertains to our aspired professional careers. To accomplish this task, we analyzed the professional field by searching through job postings in order to find common skills associated with each position. We then took to researching additional sources related to those fields for opinions on UX design written by industry-experienced professionals. From these activities we were able to compile our learnings to fully understand the importance of UX design in our future endeavors.

Project 2

This exercise entailed a design to improve the grocery shopping expirience as an improvement over the ever so popular Insta-cart or similar services. For our analysis of the current shopping environment, we created a survey and participated in several interviews to gage the main gripes of the shopping expirience. As a result, we created an app that aides college students in shopping based on selected recipes, giving a detailed walthrough of the store for each incredient.

Project 3

This exercise involved creating/modifying an existing exercise app in order to combat cheating, something that's every-so common with these types of designs. For our analysis, we performed interviews with students with a wide-range of daily activity to see what motivates them to exercise. Our final design was to designate times throughout the working day to encourage the user to get up from their seat and go on a walk. This is a form of micro-exercising, which is to encourage activity throughout the day and mitigate the effects of prolonged sitting.
